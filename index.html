About Us

About

We are an education company working towards grooming of our young students for the practical world through our academic and practical skill courses. Our academic courses are available for FREE as we envision a world where no student needs to invest their huge time & money to prepare for the degree courses.


At the same time, our practical skill courses are also available for a very nominal fee (with easy payment options) that would help you train you for the actual work to be done in the industry where you want to work.

Academic Courses

Video lcetures, study material and notes etc. for the degree courses offered by college/universites.
These courses would be available completely free.

Skill Courses

Industry specific Practical/Work skill courses as well as soft skill courses that can groom students for practical world.
These courses would be available for a very affordable fee.
